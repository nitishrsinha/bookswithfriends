Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Information We Collect

Books with Friends collects the following information when you use the app:

  • Email address and name (when you sign up or sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Books you add, including titles, authors, notes and ratings
  • Links you save
  • Voice input when you use the voice book search feature

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the app
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To allow you to share books with friends

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. The developer has technical access to the database but has no intention of reading your personal entries.

Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase (authentication and database)
  • Google Sign-In
  • Sign in with Apple
  • Open Library API (book search)

Microphone and Speech Recognition

The app uses your microphone and speech recognition only when you tap the voice button to search for books. Audio is not stored or transmitted beyond the search request.
